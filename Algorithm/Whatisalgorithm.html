What is an Algorithm?

An algorithm is a set of instructions designed to perform a task or solve a given problem.

For Example:

A recipe book is a collection of recipes, each providing step-by-step instructions to prepare food.

Let's Say You Want To Prepare Tea. The Steps Would Be:

  1. Boil water.
  2. Put tea in the teapot.
  3. Add hot water to the teapot.
  4. Pour the hot tea into teacups.
  5. Do you want to put sugar in the tea?
    • If yes, add sugar to the teacups.
    • If no, do nothing.
  6. Sit and enjoy your tea!
