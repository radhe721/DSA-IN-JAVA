What is Algorithm


An Algorithm is a set of instruction to perform a task or to solve a given problem

For Example


A recipe book is a collection of recipes in which each recipe provide an step by step instruction to prepare food


Let's Say You Want To Prepare a Tea so, The Steps would be.


    1.Boil Water
    2.Put tea in tea port
    3.Add Hot Water
    4.Put hot tea into tea cups
    5.Do You Want To Put Sugar in Tea?
      (a): If Yes, Put it into tea cups
      (b): If Not, Do Nothing
    6.Sit & Enjoy It.
